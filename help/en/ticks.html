In addition to bookmarks, TV Guide can remember single instances of a show as a form of temporary bookmark called a "tick". This is useful for one-off shows such as movies and special episodes where a permanent bookmark is not appropriate.

Click on a programme you want to tick, and select the Tick Show option from the Bookmarks menu. The show will then be shown with a tick mark wherever it appears:

Select Tick Show again to remove the tick mark. Ticks will automatically expire after 30 days.

Note that unlike bookmarks, ticks only apply to a specific showing of the programme. Repeats of the same programme later in the week will not be highlighted.
